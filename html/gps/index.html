The T-Rex Is The Best Dinosaur

General Information

Tyrannosaurus, meaning "tyrant lizard", from the Ancient Greek tyrannos, "tyrant", and sauros, "lizard", is a genus of coelurosaurian theropod dinosaur. The species Tyrannosaurus rex (rex meaning "king" in Latin), is one of the most well-represented of the large theropods.

Coloring Scheme

The T-Rex is rarely purple.

The Cretaceous‑Paleogene extinction event, also known as the Cretaceous‑Tertiary extinction, was a massive extinction.
