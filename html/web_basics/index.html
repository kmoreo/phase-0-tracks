Some Things To Consider Before Getting A Pet

  1. How much space is in your home?
  2. What is your budget?
  3. How comfortable are you with jamming your hand into your dog's mouth because she really wants to eat that catnip toy but she knows she's not allowed but she insists on testing her boundaries?
  4. ALWAYS have a cardboard box handy.
